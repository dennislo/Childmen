INITIALISE Data Store - Example POST requests:

This initalises the GAE datastore: Deletes all existing data. Adds all platforms and locations. Adds sample games, reviews and critics.


EXAMPLE Games GET requests:

click these after datastore has been initialised!

  • GET /games?outputpretty=true&name=Dead+Space+2 --- retrieve games by 'name'
  • GET /games?genre=Action --- retrieve games by 'genre'
  • GET /games?sorton=metarating&sortorder=desc --- desecending sort by 'metarating'
  • GET /games?sorton=metarating&sortorder=asc --- ascending sort by 'metarating'
  • GET /games?genre=Other+Shooters&sorton=metarating&sortorder=desc --- descending sort by 'metarating' with 'genre'
  • GET /games?genre=Other+Shooters&sorton=metarating&sortorder=asc --- ascending sort by 'metarating' with 'genre'
  • GET /games?metaratingstart=6.5&metaratingend=100.0&sorton=metarating&sortorder=desc" --- metarating range
  • GET /games?genre=Role-Playing&metarating=75.0 --- retrieve games by 'metarating'
  • GET /games?platform=PC --- retrieve by games on PC
  • GET /games?platform=all&sorton=metarating&sortorder=desc --- note: all filters ignored as sorting isn't implemented with platform

EXAMPLE Game Details GET requests:

click these after datastore has been initialised!

EXAMPLE Reviews GET requests:

click these after datastore has been initialised!

  • GET /reviews?reviewstartdate=20101125&reviewenddate=20110201 --- retrieve reviews by 'publisheddate' range
  • GET /reviews?reviewstartdate=20101125&reviewenddate=20110201&rating=2.0 --- retrieve reviews by 'publisheddate' range with 'rating'
